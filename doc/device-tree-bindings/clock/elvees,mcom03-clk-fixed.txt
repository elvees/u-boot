Bindings for ELVEES MCom-03 fixed input clocks.

See elvees,mcom03-clk.txt for MCom-03 clocks description.

Required properties:
 - compatible: Shall be "elvees,mcom03-clk-fixed"
 - #clock-cells: From common clock bindings: Shall be 0.
 - elvees,id: Shall contain ID of clock (CLK_CLK125 or CLK_I2S0_SCLK_IN).
 - clock-frequency: Shall contain fixed clock frequency.


Example:

	clk125: clk125 {
		#clock-cells = <0>;
		compatible = "elvees,mcom03-clk-fixed";
		clock-frequency = <125000000>;
		elvees,id = <CLK_CLK125>;
	};
